💡 Frequently Asked Questions
Is Patti King safe and legal to play?
▾
Yes! Patti King is 100% legal. Rummy is a skill-based game recognised by the Supreme Court of India. We use SSL encryption to protect your data and RNG-certified shuffling for fair play. All transactions are secured through PCI-DSS compliant gateways.
How do I withdraw my winnings?
▾
Go to Wallet → Winnings Tab → Withdraw. Enter the amount (minimum ₹100) and choose your bank account or UPI ID. Withdrawals are processed within 24–48 hours. Make sure your KYC is verified before withdrawing.
What documents do I need for KYC?
▾
You need: (1) Aadhaar Card for identity & address proof, (2) PAN Card for tax compliance, and (3) a selfie with your Aadhaar. KYC verification usually completes within 2–4 hours on working days.
How does the referral program work?
▾
Share your unique referral code with friends. When they register using your code AND add their first deposit of ₹50 or more, you earn ₹100 as bonus. Your friend also receives a ₹50 welcome bonus. There's no limit on the number of referrals!
I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
▾
On the login screen, tap "Login with OTP" and enter your registered mobile number or email. You'll receive a 6-digit OTP to verify yourself. Alternatively, contact our support team and we'll help you reset it within 30 minutes.
My payment failed but money was deducted. What do I do?
▾
Don't worry! If payment fails after deduction, it is automatically refunded to your source account within 5–7 business days. If you don't receive the refund, contact us via Live Chat with your transaction ID and we'll resolve it immediately.
What happens if I disconnect during a game?
▾
If you disconnect, the system holds your position for up to 60 seconds. You can rejoin by opening the app. If you are unable to rejoin, the game continues and your hand is played automatically. In case of technical issues on our end, the game is cancelled and your entry fee is refunded.
Do Bonus Coins expire?
▾
Yes, Bonus Coins are valid for 30 days from the date they are credited. They can only be used for tournament entry fees and cannot be withdrawn directly. Check the Bonus tab in your Wallet to see expiry dates.
