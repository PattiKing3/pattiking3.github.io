🏆 Our Story
Patti King is India's premier skill-based Rummy platform, built for passionate card game lovers across the country. We believe Rummy is more than a game — it's a tradition, a skill, and a way to connect.

Founded in 2021, Patti King has grown to serve over 50 lakh players across India, offering Points Rummy, Pool Rummy, Deals Rummy, and exciting Tournaments with guaranteed prize pools.

Our mission is simple: provide the most fair, secure, and entertaining Rummy experience on mobile.
